Comparion Sample Template Files

There are many Comparion project templates below you may find useful. Templates in this page only contain the structure of the project but do not contain the participant information, data and results. You can use these templates to create new projects but these templates may not necessarily be useful for demonstration purposes. For demonstration purposes, please see "Comparion Demonstration Projects" section under the Resource Center.

Please follow these steps to download templates, add them to your account and create projects based on them.
1. Click on the link to download the project template (.ahps file).
2. Click on "Decision Projects" button in Comparion, press on "New from File" button and select the file to upload the project you just downloaded.
